About This Partnership

With 20 years leading organizational transformations and five years developing Raven's Indigenous Impact Measurement framework, I bring deep expertise in leadership development and impact measurement for social purpose organizations. I help portfolio companies build the systems and capabilities to scale sustainably while delivering both financial returns and community impact.

Core Services

1. Strategic Foundation

Facilitate clarity and alignment on your organization's core identity and direction.

  • Mission, Vision, Values: Guide your team to articulate shared purpose and aspirations
  • Strategic Priorities: Identify key focus areas that advance your mission
  • OKRs Development: Translate priorities into measurable objectives and key results
  • Leadership Alignment: Ensure board and management share understanding and commitment

Often combined with Impact Measurement for integrated strategy and accountability.

2. Impact Measurement & Management

Design and implement systems to track and communicate your meaningful outcomes.

  • Theory of Change: Map pathways from activities to community outcomes
  • Impact Framework: Develop metrics aligned with Raven's Indigenous Impact approach
  • Measurement Systems: Implement tracking using Airtable, Typeform, and automation tools
  • Impact Reporting: Create compelling narratives supported by data

3. Organizational Effectiveness

  • Gap Analysis: Identify operational barriers to growth and effectiveness
  • Culture of Performance: Build systems that support both accountability and relationships
  • Team Structure Optimization: Ensure the right people are in the right roles
